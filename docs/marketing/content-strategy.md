## LinkedIn Content Calendar (Weekly)

1. **Tips posts** (1x/week) — "3 choses que tout gérant de flotte devrait surveiller"
2. **Stats posts** (2x/month) — "X% des entreprises marocaines ne savent pas où sont leurs véhicules"
3. **Feature spotlight** (1x/month) — Screenshots + demo GIF of a specific feature
4. **Customer quote** (1x/month) — Short quote from a customer (with permission)

## SEO Content Standards

Every blog post must have:

- [ ] Target keyword in H1, first paragraph, and at least 2 H2s
- [ ] Word count: 1,200–2,000 words
- [ ] Internal links to at least 2 other TrackMa pages
- [ ] One practical step-by-step section (lists, numbered steps)
- [ ] One image with alt text
- [ ] Meta description 145–155 characters with keyword
- [ ] Published in both FR and AR (AR translation in Sprint 5+)
